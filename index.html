Our Cells

Communication Cell

The Communication Cell plays a crucial role in maintaining the club's visibility, unity, and engagement, making it an essential part of JCMP ESI.

Sponsoring Cell

The Sponsoring Cell is crucial for securing the funds and resources that enable JCMP ESI to carry out its projects and events.

Media Cell

The Media Cell plays a vital role in making JCMP ESI known and appreciated by both its members and the wider community.

Organization Cell

The Organization Cell ensures that all JCMP ESI events are well-planned and executed, creating a positive experience for participants.

Treasurer Cell

The Treasurer Cell is essential for the financial health of JCMP ESI, ensuring that the club operates within its means and remains sustainable.



About us

JCMP ESI is deeply committed to humanitarian work, organizing blood drives to support hospitals, visiting orphanages to bring joy and care to children, hosting Ramadan Iftar meals at hospices to share compassion with the elderly and sick, and conducting charity caravans to deliver essential supplies and aid to remote and underserved communities in need.
